## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of my Data Analytics Internship program.

The objective of this project was to analyze e-commerce sales data, identify business trends, evaluate customer behavior, and generate actionable insights through data cleaning, descriptive statistics, and pivot table analysis.

The analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of:

- Sales performance
- Product performance
- Customer acquisition channels
- Payment preferences
- Order fulfillment trends
- Seasonal sales patterns

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis was conducted to answer the following key business questions:

### 1. Product Performance
- Which products generate the highest revenue?

### 2. Customer Acquisition
- Which referral channels attract the most customers?

### 3. Payment Analysis
- What payment methods are preferred by customers?

### 4. Sales Trend Analysis
- How does revenue fluctuate across different months?

### 5. Operational Performance
- What is the distribution of order statuses?

### 6. Statistical Analysis
- What are the key statistical characteristics of customer purchasing behavior?

To ensure accuracy and reliability, the following data cleaning procedures were performed:

### ✔ Missing Value Check
- Verified dataset for null and blank values.

### ✔ Duplicate Detection
- Checked for duplicate transaction records.

### ✔ Data Validation
- Confirmed consistency of numerical and categorical fields.

### ✔ Data Formatting
- Standardized date and currency formats.

### ✔ Data Quality Review
- Inspected records for anomalies and inconsistencies.

The dataset was transformed to improve analysis and reporting.

### Transformations Performed

- Aggregated sales data using Pivot Tables.
- Generated monthly sales summaries.
- Calculated product revenue totals.
- Summarized customer referral sources.
- Analyzed payment method usage.
- Grouped orders by status categories.

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Drivers
Top-performing products contribute a substantial share of total revenue.

### Customer Behavior
Customers show a strong preference for digital payment methods.

### Marketing Effectiveness
Instagram generated the highest number of customers among all referral channels.

### Operational Concerns
The high volume of cancelled and returned orders requires immediate attention.

### Seasonality
Sales peak during the first half of the year, particularly in June.
